Nhlakanipho Consulting & Business Development (Pty) Ltd (NKP) came about when four individuals with knowledge in law, accounting and bookkeeping, IT and Information Systems came together to put their skills and knowledge to use after working individually with a number of entrepreneurs and start-ups in offering their separate skills for the betterment and growth of these start-ups. The motivation behind coming together is to provide holistic consulting and business development solutions for entrepreneurs, start-ups and Small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs).

NKP is a start-up dedicated to providing accounting and bookkeeping, labour relations, company seceretarial services, data analysis, e-commerce platforms and other IT and business development related services to SMEs and Entrepreneurs, to help them develop systems and generate business reports that assist business owners to put controls in place to manage operational overheads in order to increase their turnover and profits while decreasing their stress and frustrations related to financial controls as well as ensuring that they remain compliant with the necessary labour relations and company legislation.
